Wind Speed by Latitude

There is no apparent correlation between wind speed and latitude. Wind is driven by differences in atmospheric pressure, which are created by a wide array of factors. As seen in the scatter plot, the only clear trend is a relative paucity of data points in the far southern latitudes, due to the relatively small amount of Earth's surface land mass and therefore cities that falls into this range.
